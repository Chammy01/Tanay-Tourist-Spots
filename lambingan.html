Lambingan Hills

  • Lambingan Hills

    Looking for a place to spend a day and relax with friends or family during the pandemic? Lambingan Hills is a campsite in Tanay, Rizal, often visited by tourists and locals, where one can experience cool climate just like in Baguio. What you need to do is make a reservation because they don’t allow walk-ins due to health and safety protocols.

  • Camping Grounds

    Lambingan Hill has a scenic view of Laguna Lake and is surrounded by verdant forest. Its main attractions are the bright red and yellow Celosia flowers.
    Aside from it, there are swings hanging on the trees for kids and kids at heart. There is a spacious camping ground for visitors who wish to spend the night.

  • Chilly Weather

    There’s nothing better to do in Lambingan Hills than simply enjoy and fall in love with nature. If you love taking photos for the gram, you’ll find several photo spots around the site, but let’s be real: Everywhere you go is worth capturing.
    Since it’s in an elevated area, expect that the weather would get chilly especially in this holiday weather. The grounds are perfect for watching the stars at night and waking up to a sea of fog in the morning. The camping grounds are also a treat for photographers who enjoy astrophotography.
