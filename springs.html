3 Springs Mountain Park

  • 3 Springs Mountain Park

    3 Springs Mountain Park is a private venue and outdoor park that is perfect for day visits and overnight vacations. There are no hotel rooms available here. The major attraction is camping beneath the stars in a beautiful setting overlooking the Sierra Madre mountain range. This is an excellent introduction for first-timers who wish to have an enjoyable camping experience.

  • Camp Site

    There are designated camping areas throughout the property. The main camping area right next to the meadows is very near the multi-purpose hall, shower area and clean and well-maintained comfort rooms.

  • Small Activities

    The grassy sloped meadow serves as the main team-building area and gaming grounds where visitors can experience trying traditional Filipino games like patintero and kaba-kabayuhan. You can also bring kites, balls, frisbees and other sporting equipment to play here. Be sure to bring a guitar, playing cards, board games and lots of chips and snacks. We got engrossed in the afternoon just playing card games, with a brief stop to take photos of the sunset.

  • Dining

    Unlike the standard tapsilog / bulalo type meals you can find in other road trip restaurants along Marilaque, 3 Springs Mountain Park specializes in healthier fare using fresh herbs and spices from their garden. Everything we had was really good. For merienda, we had this Aglio Olio pasta that looked very simple, but tasted so good especially with the addition of fresh basil.
